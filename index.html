The specialists guiding and performing your treatment.

Marc Lussier, MD, FACS

Medical Director · Plastic Surgeon

Dr. Lussier is a board-certified plastic surgeon and the founder and medical director of Lussier Aesthetics. Trained at NYU, Baylor, and USC, with over 22 years in practice and his own surgical center in Valencia, he has been ranked the #1 Cosmetic Surgeon in the Santa Clarita Valley every year since 2011.

His philosophy across both surgery and non-surgical aesthetics is the same: do less, more precisely, so the result looks natural — not done. Every aesthetic protocol at the practice is reviewed and directed by him.

Claire Bennett, RN

Nurse Injector · Aesthetic Lead

Claire brings a depth most aesthetic practices can't match. After 17 years in emergency and trauma medicine, she pivoted into aesthetics and trained extensively under nationally recognized injectors.

Her clinical background gives her a sharp eye for facial anatomy and a particularly steady hand for first-time patients. She specializes in conservative neuromodulator dosing and natural-looking lip work — patients consistently describe her results as "the kind nobody can quite put their finger on."

Jade Gonzales, RN, BSN

Nurse Injector · Skin Specialist

Jade has been a registered nurse since 2009 and has specialized in aesthetic medicine since 2015. She trained with leading injectors across Southern California and built her career around two crafts: artistic lip filler and radiofrequency microneedling.

Patients who want both injectables and skin resurfacing in a single visit gravitate to Jade — she sees the face as a whole, not a checklist of areas, and plans treatments accordingly.
